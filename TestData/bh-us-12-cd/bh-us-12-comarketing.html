Co-Branded Email Invitations

Black Hat has partnered with Exhibitor Invites to provide you the opportunity to send your customers and prospects and co-branded email inviting them to your booth and offering a $100 discount on Briefings Passes.

To deploy, you'll upload your logo, company information, and contact list into Exhibitor Invites, a tool that will automatically generate a co-branded email for your review and approval. The emails will be deployed to your list from Exhibitor Invites, a bonded third party mail house.

Deploy a co-branded email to a minimum of 250 qualified customers or prospects on or before each of the specified dates to accumulate multiple rewards:
