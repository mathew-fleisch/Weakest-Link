CFP Terms

Papers and presentations will open on April 1, 2012 for the Black Hat USA 2012 Briefings. NOTE: CFP for Black Hat USA 2012 closes on May 15, 2012.

GENERAL NOTES

  • I understand that I MAY NOT submit presentations if I am a third party such as a PR firm or Speaker Representative that wishes to submit ../materials on behalf of a potential speaker. I understand that only original authors of presentations may submit for the Call for Papers.
  • I understand that I will be notified of the status (acceptance or denial) no later than June 8, 2012.
  • I understand that full conference passes are given to those who actively participate as speakers on accepted presentations. The speakers must be listed as a part of the submitted Call for Papers applications PRIOR to the close of the CFP. These passes are non-transferable. Co-workers, PR persons, press, etc. who wish to attend the session or conference must register and pay the appropriate conference fees.
  • I understand that I may edit the submission application until the close of the Call for Papers.
  • I understand that Black Hat reserves the right to rescind an accepted submission up to 48 hours after the acceptance without reason.
  • I understand that Black Hat reserves the right to rescind an accepted submission at ANY TIME should the presentation be deemed to be a vendor sales pitch or if the content is found to be a work of plagiarism.
  • I understand that the Black Hat Review Board will have access to review my submitted application and ../materials.
  • I understand that submissions are reviewed as they come in and Acceptances and/or declines are conducted on a rolling basis.
  • I agree to not contact any Black Hat Review Board members directly regarding the status of my submission or feedback or lack thereof. All correspondence and queries that I have regarding my submission will be sent to cfp@blackhat.com
  • I understand that if I am completing a submission via the Black Hat Call for Papers system that I will have read, understand and agree to the terms as detailed in this section and subsequent sections of this document.

GRANT OF COPYRIGHT USE

  • I warrant that the submitted work has not been previously published elsewhere, or if it has, that I have obtained permission for its publication by Black Hat and that I will promptly supply Black Hat with wording for crediting the original publication and copyright owner.
  • If I am selected for presentation, I hereby give Black Hat permission to duplicate, record and redistribute this presentation; including, but not limited to, the conference proceedings, conference CD, video, audio, hand outs(s) to the conference attendees for educational, on-line and all other purposes.

I have read the above and understand that by completing the Call for Papers application that I agree to the terms as detailed in the Grant of Copyright Use.

AGREEMENT TO TERMS OF SPEAKING REQUIREMENTS

If I am selected to speak at the Briefings, I understand that I must complete and fulfill the following requirements:

  • I will submit a photo headshot for use in promotional and marketing ../materials within 72 hours of being accepted or I will forfeit $250 of my honorarium.
  • I will submit a completed White Paper for publication on the website and conference CD on Friday, July 13, 17:00 PST. I understand if I fail to submit a completed white paper by July 13, I may be replaced by an alternate presentation or, if allowed to present, will forfeit $250 of my honorarium.
  • I will submit a completed presentation for publication on the website and conference CD on Friday, July 13, 17:00 PST. I understand if I fail to submit a completed presentation by July 13, I will forfeit $250 of my honorarium.
  • If my submission included the release of a tool, I understand that I will submit a working version of the tool for publication on the website and conference CD on Friday, July 13, 17:00 PST. I understand if I fail to submit the tool I will forfeit $250 of my honorarium.
  • I will include an appropriate slide (I may opt to use the Black Hat provided slide) at the beginning and end of the presentation that will include the following information: Please complete the Speaker Feedback Surveys.
    This will help speakers to improve and for Black Hat to make better decisions regarding content and presenters for future events.
  • I will include a detailed bibliography as either a separate document, for inclusion on the Black Hat website or conference cd, included within the presentation of all resources cited and/or used in my presentation.
  • I will be on hand and accessible to delegates during Black Hat social events including, but not limited to, luncheons, breaks and receptions. I will be scheduled to speak at a specific time and date but understand that I must be available to speak at ANY time on all three days of the Briefings should there be a schedule change.
  • If applicable, I will submit updated/revised/edited versions of the presentation, white paper, and a copy of the tool(s) and/or code(s), and a reference to all of the tool(s), law(s), Web sites and/or publications referenced to at the end of my talk and as described in this CFP submission for publication on the Black Hat website no later than Friday, July 26, 17:00 PST.
  • I will complete my presentation in the time allocated to me - not ending 15 minutes before or running 5 minutes over the time allocation. If I do, I will forfeit the appropriate calculated percentage of my honorarium. Example: My speaking slot is 60 minutes. I only speak for 30 minutes. I will forfeit 50% of my honorarium.
  • I understand that Black Hat will provide 1 LCD projector, 1 screen, wired LAN and wired microphone.
  • I understand that I am responsible for providing all other necessary equipment, including laptops and machines, to complete my presentation.
  • I understand that only persons listed as "speakers" who were part of the submission prior to the close of the CFP and who actively participate in giving the presentation will be given entry to the conference. Co-workers, PR persons, etc. who wish to attend the session must register and pay the appropriate conference fees.
  • I understand that if the presentation is deemed to be a vendor pitch then I will waive my honorarium.
  • I agree to not disparage the Black Hat brand for the duration of this contract (date of Letter of Acceptance to date of payment of honorarium). Any disparaging comments will result in forfeiture of payment including the honorarium and reimbursement for travel.
  • I understand and agree that I will not present the accepted submission at another conference or event, public or private, from the date of my acceptance through July 26, 2012, 11:59 PST.

I have read the above and understand that by completing the Call for Papers application that I agree to the terms as detailed in the Agreement to Terms of Speaking Requirements.

AGREEMENT TO TERMS OF SPEAKING REMUNERATION

I understand that remuneration will be based on one of the following categories:

Category 1: Briefings Presenter (60 minute):

  • You have never previously presented at Black Hat before
  • Black Hat will pay for one presenter's coach class round-trip airfare and one hotel room for three nights at the Conference Hotel and a speaking honorarium of $500 USD.

Category 2: Briefings Presenter (60 minute):

  • You have spoken before at Black Hat (either as a Trainer, Briefings Presenter or Webcast Presenter) or you have been specifically invited to speak by the Event Director.
  • Black Hat will pay for one presenter's coach class round-trip airfare and one hotel room for three nights at the Conference Hotel and a speaking honorarium of $1000 USD.

Category 3: Turbo Talk Presenter (15 minute):

  • No airfare, honorarium or accommodations are offered or reimbursed
  • Gratis entry to the event for a single presenter per turbo presentation
  • Gratis entry is granted to the primary speaker only.

Category 4: Briefings Workshop Presenter (120/180 minute):

  • You have specifically applied for a workshop slot
  • Black Hat will pay for one presenter's coach class round-trip airfare and one hotel room for three nights at the Conference Hotel and a speaking honorarium of $2000 USD for a 120 minute Workshop or $3000 for a 180 minute workshop

Category 5: Sponsor Employed Presenters (60 minute):

  • I understand if I want Black Hat to pay for one presenter's coach class round-trip airfare and one hotel room for three nights at the Conference Hotel and a speaking honorarium ($500 USD for new Black Hat presenters, $1000 USD for returning Black Hat presenters) then I MUST abide by and meet the following guidelines:
    • My presentation is not a vendor pitch.
    • I MUST use the Black Hat provided slide template.
    • I will ONLY use my employer logo on the following two slides:
      • Introduction
      • Biography
    • I will only use my employer's name in relation to my work, demo, or tool and NOT use it is as a product pitch or product sale.
  • I understand that if the company I work for or myself do not agree and fail to meet the above (including the use my company mandated slide deck) then I WAIVE the right to any compensation for travel, accommodations or honorarium.
  • I understand that if the presentation is deemed to be a vendor pitch then I will waive my honorarium.

Remuneration:

  • I understand that honorarium and travel reimbursements will be made at the same time, net 45 after the last day of the conference. This means the soonest I may expect payment is after September 5.
  • I understand that if I fail to provide my travel receipts by Friday, July 20, 17:00 PST, I forfeit any claims for reimbursement.
  • I understand that only a single payment will be made per presentation and that payment will be made to the primary presenter (the person who submitted the original application) and that hotel and airfare will also be made for the primary presenter only. Co-presenters will be fiscally and logistically responsible for their own travel and lodging arrangements.
  • I understand that payment will be made via check for persons residing in the US. Checks will be issued from UBM – United Business Media.
  • I understand that if I am not a US Citizen and do not reside in the United States that payment will be made via bank wire transfer. I understand that it is my responsibility to provide accurate bank details and that any transaction fees billed to me by my bank will be borne by myself. I will not seek reimbursement from Black Hat - UBM to cover any banking fees. Bank wire transactions will be reflected as UBM- United Business Media.
  • I understand that if I choose to book my own air travel and wish to be reimbursed the following terms apply:
    • I will be limited to coach class fares
    • There is a cap of $1500 USD on round itineraries
    • There is a cap of $500 USD on one-way itineraries
    • Itinerary must be round trip with arrival to the venue city for the event
    • Itinerary must be booked no later than June 21 or within 2 weeks of my Letter of Acceptance (whichever is sooner).
    • I will provide a valid receipt in the form of a travel agency issued air itinerary and/or ticket receipt with the airfare and itinerary with date of purchase printed on it.
  • I understand that Black Hat uses American Express Corporate Travel services for booking airfare. If I choose to have Black Hat book my air travel, that they will make a best effort to book based on my preferred itinerary however the itinerary may not be available via the Black Hat corporate travel department. I can choose to either book the fare myself (based on the guidelines regarding booking my own air travel) or will agree to an alternate itinerary as provided by Black Hat.

I understand that I will not be reimbursed for parking, taxi or shuttle services surrounding the event.
