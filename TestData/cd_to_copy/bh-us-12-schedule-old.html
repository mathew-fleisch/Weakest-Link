A full schedule will be posted after June 10. Our Call for Papers closed on May 15 and many Briefings are still under review.

Black Hat USA 2012 will again feature 9 tracks during the Briefings which will consist of a mix of workshops, round tables/panels and presentations. Attendees are free to move between each session and tracks. The Trainings on the four days before the start of the Briefings (July 21-24) are a separate line items and not included in the Briefings price.

Continental breakfast, coffee service (2 per day) and lunch is included with all Briefings passes. There is also a cocktail Speaker & Review Board Meet-and-Greet on the evening of July 25 open to all paid Briefings delegates as well (tentatively set for 18:30-20:00, final time TBA after July 1).
